Hey guys welcome to my channel today I’m going to tell you how to make chocolates in a easy and simple way .

Ingredients: 1. milk chocolate 2. chocolate moulds 3. toppings (are optional so it’s your wish if you only use them )

Procedure: 1. We are going to use the double boiling method. 2. First take 1/3 vessel water and put is on the stove and allow it to boil. 3. After it boils turn off the stove and set the vessel aside. 4. Now take 150 grams of compound milk chocolate in a heat resistant bowl and start melting it in such a way that the heat resistant bowl does not touch the water. 5. Use a spatula or whisk and keep mixing the chocolate till it comes to a silky smooth consistency. 6. If you want to add any toppings you have to add it in this stage because you can’t add it later. You can use any toppings chocolate chips, vanilla chips, sprinkles etc. 7. After you add the sprinkles mix the whole melted chocolate. 8. Now take a spoon and start pouring chocolate into the moulds. 9. After pouring the chocolate into the moulds put it in the fridge and freeze it for one and a half hour. 10. Voila, Your chocolate is done Un-mold it and enjoy .
